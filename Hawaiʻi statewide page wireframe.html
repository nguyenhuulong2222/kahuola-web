Kahu Ola Civic Doctrine — Statewide Dashboard
🔥 Wildfire-first monitoring remains active. Fire is the highest-priority signal class. This statewide flood/storm context does not reduce wildfire vigilance. NASA FIRMS satellites scan every ~90 minutes; any hotspot escalates immediately.
🌧 Storm and flood are statewide context signals. Each island's specific conditions are shown in the island grid. Flood Watch means conditions support possible flooding — a Warning means flooding is occurring or imminent. Follow NWS guidance for exact levels.
🏛 Official links always take priority. Kahu Ola provides situational awareness only. All emergency orders, evacuation directives, and official guidance come from county, state, and federal authorities. This platform does not issue emergency orders.
⚡ Graceful degraded state when data is stale. If any source feed is unavailable, the panel shows "Monitoring" as a fallback — never a blank screen. Stale data is always labeled with its age. The UI renders under all known failure conditions.
🔒 Zero PII · Zero tracking. No location data ever leaves your device. No accounts. No ads. No behavioral tracking. Privacy is a design principle, not a feature.
